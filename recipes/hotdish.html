Hotdish

Description

This recipe for a tasty macaroni hotdish is sure to keep everyone in the family coming back for seconds!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb elbow macaroni
  • 1 lb hamburger
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 Tbsp garlic
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • seasonings

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the elbow macaroni noodles and cook until al dente, draining water when finished.
  2. While the macaroni is cooking, brown hamburger on a pan on the stovetop.
  3. Brown hamburger on medium heat.
  4. After the hamburger is browned, dice the onion and add it to the browned hambuger.
  5. In a large casserole dish, combine all ingredients and combine thoroughly.
  6. Bake hotdish in oven at 375F for 30 minutes or until a crispy brown crust forms over the top layer.
