Buffalo Chicken Drumsticks

Description

I LOVE the drummies.

Ingredients

  • 4 mid sized chicken drumsticks
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • flour
  • buffalo sauce

Steps

  1. Season the chicken drumsticks by placing them in a large mixing bowl with a lid, adding all ingredients, and shaking thoroughly.
  2. Place the breaded drumsticks in an air fryer, and cook for 30 minutes at 360F.
  3. Remove cooked drumsticks from air fryer and toss in buffalo sauce.
