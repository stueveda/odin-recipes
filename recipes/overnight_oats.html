Overnight Oats

Description

This hearty breakfast recipe is easy to prep overnight and will be a great jumpstart to your day!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 Tbsp plain greek yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1 handful blueberries

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a sealable jar and mix well.
  2. Place jar in refrigerator for at least 8 hours and up to 3 days.
  3. Remove from fridge and eat oats directly from jar.
